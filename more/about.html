About ProviStar

SK Shipping company operates in the field of general ship supply since 2005. Main office and service station of SK Shipping company are situated in the centre of Nikolaev (Ukraine) equidistant to all ports of the region.

Fast and efficiently we'll take care of your orders. Your ship’s crew can enjoy a broad variety of excellent quality products. Our aim is to satisfy your needs providing you reliable services at affordable prices any time. Our team is working on full time basis and 24 hours a day to encounter the demands of our clients.

Whatever you may need we can provide it to you.

Among our values are:

Professionalism

We provide services and solutions to ensure your satisfaction. That's why we are open to new ideas and approaches. SK Shipping follows customers and partners feedbacks attentively as we understand how important evaluation is.

Responsibility

We take full responsibility for our services and products we supply you with. Today and in the future it is important for us to maintain a company’s business on friendly and financially viable basis.

Flexibility

Our work is absolutely clear and open. At SK Shipping we always react instantly to emerging challenges. Our company is customer oriented and always ready to provide best solutions, whenever our clients consider it necessary.
